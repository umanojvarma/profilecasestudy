Interactive AI Case Studies

TaxEase: AI-Powered Tax Filing Assistant (AI Hackathon Winner)

Problem: Tax filing is complex, time-consuming, and often leads to missed savings opportunities for individuals.

Solution: Conceptualized, built, and launched "TaxEase," an AI-powered assistant designed to simplify personal tax filings.

  • Utilizes Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze Form 16 and other financial documents.
  • Identifies potential tax savings, predicts returns, and provides actionable gap analyses.
  • Showcased innovative application of multi-agent architectures (Langflow) for task decomposition.
  • Demonstrated expertise in LLM tuning and prompt engineering to deliver precise financial guidance.
Impact & Results:

Recognized for identifying an average of 15-20% in potential savings opportunities for users. The project validated the potential for market disruption and strong Product-Market Fit through AI-driven financial optimization.

Key Technologies: Generative AI Multi-Agent Architectures (Langflow) LLM Tuning Prompt Engineering Financial Modeling NLP

CompeteBot: Agentic AI Competitive Analysis Tool

Problem: Product managers spend weeks on manual competitive research, resulting in outdated and inefficient intelligence.

Solution: Designed and developed "CompeteBot," a multi-agent AI system on Langflow that automates and accelerates competitive analysis.

  • Conducted deep unmet needs analysis and persona definition (Senior Product Manager) to ensure Product-Market Fit.
  • Orchestrated three specialized AI agents: Planner, Researcher, and Executor for task decomposition, intelligence gathering from diverse sources (web APIs, documents), and synthesis.
  • Generates decision-ready outputs including tables, SWOT analyses, and strategic recommendations.
  • Built with scalable, ethical AI solutions as a core foundation for a robust Go-to-Market strategy.
Impact & Results:

Achieved an 80% reduction in analysis time (from 2-3 weeks to 2-3 hours) and demonstrated a 40% productivity gain for the target persona, delivering consistent, audited results with 95% consistency.

Key Technologies: Agentic AI Multi-Agent System (Langflow) Web Scraping APIs Document Analysis Product-Market Fit Value Proposition Design
